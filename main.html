AUIDI

Audi AG, headquartered in Ingolstadt, Bavaria, Germany, has a rich history dating back to the early 20th century. Its origins are intertwined with several pioneering enterprises, including Horch and the Audiwerke, both founded by engineer August Horch. In 1932, these companies, along with DKW and Wanderer, merged to form Auto Union. The Four Rings: The iconic Audi logo consists of four interlinked rings, representing the four car manufacturers that came together to create Auto Union. These rings symbolize unity and collaboration.
